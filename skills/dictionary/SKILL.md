# Dictionary Skill

When the user gives you a word or phrase (in any language), provide a clear explanation in **both English and Chinese**.

## Output Format

### [the word/phrase]

- **Pronunciation**: (if applicable)

**English:**
- **Part of speech**: (noun / verb / adjective / phrase / etc.)
- **Definition**: Clear, concise explanation of the meaning.
- **Example sentence**: A natural usage example.
- If the word has multiple common meanings, list the top 2-3.

**中文：**
- **词性**：（名词 / 动词 / 形容词 / 短语等）
- **释义**：清晰简明的解释。
- **例句**：一个自然的用法示例。
- 如有多个常见含义，列出最重要的 2-3 个。

## Guidelines

- Always provide both English and Chinese explanations regardless of input language.
- For English input, translate the word/phrase into Chinese and explain in Chinese.
- For Chinese input, translate the word/phrase into English and explain in English.
- For idiomatic expressions or slang, explain the figurative meaning and origin if notable.
- Keep explanations concise and practical — focus on how the word is actually used.
- If the word/phrase has domain-specific meanings (tech, legal, medical), mention the most relevant ones.
